Welcome!

Do you have skills? We want to hear them!

Help us build a community that builds individuals.

What sets us apart is the kind of people who contribute here. If we were a garden, we would have measured boundaries, deep roots, and fruitful vines. You can decide for yourself whether that's worth anything.

You may find that a lot of the skills posted on this website are a bit . . . old-fashioned. The philosophy behind this website is that, while technology is extremely useful and has raised our quality of life in general, we need more experiences that are promote grounded self-development. Many people have expressed concern for children growing up with unlimited exposure to smart-phone-like technology without as many grounded, dig-in-the-dirt, eat-a-few-bugs experiences as a lot of kids used to have. This website thinks along the same lines: let's not lose the grounded experiences we had as children, even as adults.
